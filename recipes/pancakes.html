Good Old Fashioned Pancake Recipe

This is a classic pancake recipe for 8 servings. It will take about a total of 20 minutes to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add and mix the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
  2. Make a hole in the center and add the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake on frying pan.
  6. Cook both sides and serve hot.
