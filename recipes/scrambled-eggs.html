Fluffy Scrambled Eggs Recipe

This scrambled egg recipe is for 2 servings. It will take about a total of 10 minutes to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 teaspoons butter

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs with salt until completely combined and foamy.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over low heat until bubbling.
  3. Add whisked eggs.
  4. Stirr frequently until thickened and creamy; 2-3 min.
  5. Remove scrambled eggs from skillet.
  6. Serve.
